Cardmaster Showdown

Rizing Powers of Zyrex · 1v1 TCG · 9-card teams · 27 gems · Draft your roster, command the field, claim the saga.

House rules

  • A1 — 1 gem, ATK ×0.33, always goes first.
  • A2 — 2 gems, ATK ×0.66, SPD-ordered.
  • A3 — 3 gems, ATK ×0.99, dice roll required (must beat opponent's roll).
  • SPC — free, one-time. Adds 25% of SPC value to one chosen stat permanently.
  • DEF reduction — 5 brackets (10/20/30/40/50%), capped at DEF 300.
  • FALLEN at 0 HP. Reserve shifts in. If shifted card SPD > opponent's, it also attacks this turn.
  • Revive costs equal the tier of the card (T1=1g … T5=5g) and returns at 50% HP.
