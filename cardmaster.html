Rules · Cardmaster Showdown

Per-card gems. Every card enters play carrying 1 gem. Gems live on the card, not in a shared pool. Spend a turn on Draw to bank +1 gem toward that card’s abilities.

Each turn, pick ONE.

  • A1 — free light attack. 33% ATK. Always available.
  • Draw — skip your attack to bank +1 gem on the card.
  • A2 — 2 gems. Stat swing: boost one of your stats or weaken the opponent’s by 11% × tier. Repeatable.
  • A3 — 3 gems. Heavy: 99% ATK damage + a self stat swing + inflicts a random status. Once per match.

The gamble. Trade A1-for-A1 (safe, small) or spend turns drawing gems — you eat free hits while you set up the bigger A2 / A3 payoff.

Astralite status effects. Gems run on unstable astralites. A3 inflicts one, and spending gems has a 15% chance of backfiring on you: Overcook (HP burn), Undershock (−33% ability damage), Brainlock (A1 only), Gridfreeze (skip a turn), Souldrift (stats fade — lethal).

Opening. One 1d6 roll decides who picks PLAY (act first) or PASS. After that, turns alternate.

DEF reduction. Five brackets (10 / 20 / 30 / 40 / 50%), capped at DEF 300.

Switch. Bring in any reserve. If its SPD beats the opponent’s active card, it also attacks this turn.

Win. Defeat all 9 of your opponent’s cards.
