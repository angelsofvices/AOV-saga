Book II

The First Hedonistic Era

The Age of Interaction
GovernanceEarly Influence

Immortals begin interacting with humanoid experience. First spread of blood rituals — Malrec's first ritual sits here. Mirror-pair with the Second Hedonistic Era (856 — 908 CE); both precede a War Era.

Book II · III
135 — 305 CE

The Era of Families

The Age of Kingdoms
GovernanceRegional Rule

Dominion families form. Early kingdoms emerge. The saga's most populous early-character era; the Aur-Veridae line rises through this stretch.

Book III
305 — 333 CE

The Era of Fire & Blood

The Age of Tension
GovernanceFragmented Powers

Political instability and corruption escalate. The pre-Blood-War cascade. Culminates in Audrelius X's unification at 333 CE.

I · The Blood War
333 — 346 CE

The Blood War

The First War of Viridia · Rise of the Order
GovernanceOrder of the People

Audrelius Veridae X unites the five regions and ends fragmented rule. The Order of the People is founded — Viridia's first global governance. The Aur- line becomes the canonical bloodline of unity.

Book IV Audrelius Veridae X
346 — 400 CE

The Reconstruction Era

The Age of Restoration · The Bridge Act
GovernanceOrder of the People

Viridia rebuilt; centralised governance stabilised. Hollowink origin (Rook Sablehollow, 399 CE) seeded here.

400 — 502 CE

The Unity Era · Industrialisation Period

Infrastructure · Trade · Districts
GovernanceOrder of the People

Infrastructure, trade, and systems expand; districts connect. Central Republic + Central Defense formed.

502 — 732 CE

The Unity Era · Culturalisation Period

Five Regions Become One Nation
GovernanceOrder of the People

Viridia transitions from five regions into one unified nation. ASTRAGLASS discovered (600 CE Coilglass) — saga tech origin. The first Nine (Praised A4 humanoids) emerge.

732 — 856 CE

The Unity Era · Harmonisation Period

Peak Stability
GovernanceOrder of the People

Peak balance and stability. Unity normalised. Hidden corruption resurges in the tail (Azorak 666 CE bone-engineering; Vesha Grimvoid 741 CE).

856 — 908 CE

The Second Hedonistic Era

The Age of Excess
GovernanceOrder Weakening

Society declines into indulgence; discipline collapses. The OOP starts to lose grip. Mirror-pair with the First Hedonistic Era (100 — 135 CE).

II · The Immortal War
908 — 1000 CE

The Immortal War

The Second War of Viridia · Veilfall (1st) · Fall of the Order
GovernanceOrder of the People

First open battle of the Virtue Immortals (V+) against the Vice Immortals (V−) on Viridian soil. Emperor Audrelius and King Amyros are assassinated. Queen Empress Amyra Silverstone-Veridae inherits a fracturing world. Imperion stands against Odion in defense of Mira — a Vice protecting a Virtue. The Veil tears; the Aur- bloodline goes dark for one thousand years.

The Age of Renewal
GovernanceOVV

The Order of Virtue & Vices replaces the OOP at 1000 CE (Solaris compliance trigger). Regulated balance after the Second War. The Aur- bloodline runs dark for the full 502 years.

The Age of Discovery Beyond
GovernanceOVV

Technology enables exploration beyond the stars. Pearl Regent Isolde (1600) is among The Nine. Ends six years before Stultor inherits Hollowink (1666).

1672 — 1865 CE

The Cosmic Inquiry Era

The Age of Questioning
GovernanceOVV

Society questions life beyond Viridia. Mortals process having watched Immortals ascend and descend the Veil during the Second War.

Book VII lead-in
1865 — 1998 CE

The Era of the Falling Skies

The Age of Celestial Proof
GovernanceOVV

Astral relics fall from the sky, confirming higher realms (Virtua & Infernia). The last A4 Nine (Radiant Vicar Soren 1890) walks the world. Stormvale Period onset — Carl Stormvale II born 1968; President Lezlie Tahiti Stormvale born 1971.

The Modern Eon · 1998 — 2031 CE
1998 — 2007 CE

The Supernova Era

Aetherfall Discovery · The Hero-Villain Age
GovernanceOVV

Discovery of the Pre-Creation Archive revealing the Divine Flaws. Urban Mori outbreaks intensify. Valstorm (Carl Stormvale III, 1998) and The Mech (Makai Stormvale, 2003) born. Haven's Gate becomes the institution that defines the age.

Book VII

The Modernization Era

The Age of Hidden Truth
GovernanceOVV

Knowledge is studied, suppressed, reshaped. Andre Hart born 2020 CE — the saga-culminating final entry, pre-Veilfall. The bookshop kid will become Auraxion.

Book VIII build-up Andre Hart born
III · The Equinox War

The Equinox War

The Third War of Viridia · Veilfall (2nd)
GovernanceOVV (challenged)

One thousand years of bloodline silence ends. Andre Hart unlocks the Book of Viridia. Khronicore takes the Throne of Darkness. Andre rises against Khronis, becomes Auraxion, and seals him back into the Book. The Equinox System replaces the OVV. The path to the Aethryx Expanse opens.

Book VIII Andre Hart → Auraxion
2031 CE

Equinox Era Begins

The Age of Balance Restored
GovernanceEquinox System

War ends. Balance between order and chaos is achieved. The Equinox System replaces the OVV — the saga's final mortal governance on Viridia. The Aetherstride is built. Bk IX opens with the path to the Aethryx Expanse already laid.
