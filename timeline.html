~15 Billion Years Ago

The Aenor Eruption

The Cosmic Big Bang

Aenor erupts — the cosmic substrate of the Aethryx Expanse is forged. The eruption births 1,000+ original Dracolords: the first macro-lifeforms of the cosmos, born of stellar plasma, astralite matter, dimensional gravity, cosmic consciousness, and primordial celestial law. Larger than moons. Able to cross galaxies. The conditions for everything that follows are set in this single event.

First 1 Billion Years

The Great Dying

Of 1,000 Dracolords, only six survive

Across the first billion years, nearly all the original Dracolords fall. Some collapse into stars and become suns. Some evolve beyond dimensions, passing out of the Expanse entirely. Some self-consume in substrate-burnout. Some go dormant in cosmic-time hibernation. Some destroy one another in the pre-Accord internecine wars. Only six remain. They are one family.

~14 Billion Years Ago

The Six Cosmic Guardians

One family · all dragonkind in the Expanse descends from them

Alphaea — one of the original 1,000+ and one of the six survivors — mated once with Anciuxor (the Father, not a Dracolord). Their one direct child was Azyrath. Azyrath then mothered the other four through the Great Dying, sheltering them until each could take a quadrant-zone of the Expanse.

~13 Billion Years Ago

The Eternal Accord

Six chose balance over domination

The six survivors form the Eternal Accord. The Accord founds dimensional stability · astralite equilibrium · cosmic law · planetary sustainability · the Cosmic Mirror Principle itself. Through the Accord, the six evolve from dragons into Cosmic Guardians — each retaining both a dragon form and a humanoid form. All dragonkind across the Expanse descends from this founding family. This is why dragons are universally held sacred: emblems of divinity, cosmic law, and transcendence.

The shadow returnsA purple gem from Origon — a Father-Gem fragment that long ago fell to Draevos — corrupted Elzoran into Omegoran. When Omegoran was purged, the shadow essence that drove the corruption escaped and made for Origon, the only world dense enough to hold it whole. On Origon's high-density substrate the shadow coalesces into a new cosmic-being: Eurakeon. He has come home in the gem's sense — back to the source the fragment was cut from.

Voltyran's Defense★ VOLTYRAN vs EURAKEON ★ — Voltyran, Origon-native, rises to defend its homeland. The 2-tier asymmetry (Voltyran VI · Eurakeon VIII) is partially closed by Voltyran's home-field cosmic-substrate advantage — the Father Gem core amplifies natives' effective combat-tier into the VII—VIII range on Origon's surface. Outcome: Voltyran EXPELS Eurakeon from Origon. Eurakeon does not die — he is cast off-world, denied the source-substrate. He drifts onward and eventually settles on Zyraxis, where the cosmic-substrate density is close enough to the original Father-Gem register for him to take a permanent seat — as a Gemlord.

I · THE BLOOD WAR

The Blood War

The First War of Viridia · Rise of the Order

Pre-War Context

The Era of Fire & Blood precedes the war. Regional corruption builds; Sylvaerys's corruption in the South catalyses the conflict. The fragmented regional structure inherited from Audrelius VIII's Civic Crown era reaches breaking point — Central Veridae rule is challenged across multiple regions. The cosmic-political infrastructure built up across centuries (V Hunting Roads → VI Statutory Codes → VII Religious-Cultural Unity → VIII Civic Crown → IX Royal Roads) is the war-mobilization substrate Audrelius X inherits.

The War

Mortal-tier conflict — the Immortals do not directly fight. Audrelius X Veridae is the first centre-protagonist of the Aur line and was never trained for war. ~13 years of regional combat unfold in a pattern of regional rebellion → Central counter-mobilization via Royal Roads → decisive battles → political consolidation under a new imperial register.

Key Beats

  • Battle of Civic Square — the war's climactic engagement; cements New Viridia's political centre
  • Multi-region campaign sequence — South (Sylvaerys), and counter-mobilizations across North + East
  • Northern alliance secured with help from Aelion Everfrost (Elven King of Auroravale) and Aetheon-influenced Northern magic

The War

The Khronicore origin lock (v9.6 patch 39): Onyaxius — the Shadowyng Dragon, the Onyx Moon (#347) — is the most recently hatched dragon in Viridia. Excidior's soul-economy gift corrupts Onyaxius into KHRONICORE (V Apex → VI Pseudoimmortal · tier upgrade via cosmic-corruption mechanic). Excidior delivers the corrupted dragon to Khronis as his mount. The cosmic-villain pair is complete.
