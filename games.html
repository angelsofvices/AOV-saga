Two cards meet in the cosmic arena. The saga learning to fight.

A first taste of Rizing Powers TCG mechanics. Real card stats. Real combat math. Choose A1, A2, or A3 attacks at gem cost. Watch DEF tiers reduce damage. Race for SPD initiative. The simplest version of the cosmic battle system that will one day power the full TCG.

The Battlegrounds

Full TCG · 3v3 Match

The true Rizing Powers experience — three Rizers per side, gem economy, the gamble rule, revivals, and the strategic depth the full TCG was built for.

Pick your champion, two random allies join. Each side starts with 9 gems. Commit to attacks — A1/A2/A3 raise your wager. When a Rizer falls, their wager transfers to your opponent's pool. Bring fallen Rizers back at half health for 5 gems. Win by wiping the team or by draining their gems dry. A real strategy game inside the cosmic arena.

Mobile · Desktop ~5 minutes Champion select 41 playable cards

The Expedition

Exploration RPG · Viridia

Walk the world of Viridia. Meet the Rizers. Chart the cosmos one footstep at a time.

A 2D top-down expedition through Viridia, the first world of The AOV Saga. Explore a 40×40 tile region of grasslands, towns, and the frozen north. Encounter the four founding Rizers — Voltyran, Eurakeon, Aurexion, and Elzoran — each with their own cries and cosmic role. Board the Aetherstride UFO terminal, manage your faction roster, and chart a course across the Aethryx Expanse. The first taste of the full open-world saga.
