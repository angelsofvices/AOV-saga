What Is Viridia?

Viridia is Planet 27 of the Aethryx Expanse — known across the cosmos as The Final World. Where other worlds were shaped by crystal mutation, war, or cosmic collapse, Viridia evolved through something rarer: civilization, emotion, belief, and the eternal balance between creation and destruction.

It is suspended between fantasy, science, spirituality, and destiny. Ancient dragons soar across celestial mountain ranges. Immortals guide kingdoms from beyond the Veil. Astral energy — the living cosmic force that defines power, evolution, and consciousness — flows through every grain of soil, every heartbeat, every thought.

The stronger one's astral density,
the closer they move toward
legend, divinity, or corruption.

Five continental regions. Fifty Astral States. Three world-shattering wars that reshaped reality itself. Above its skies rise the immortal realms of Virtua and Infernia. Below its crust spreads the corruption of Khronexus. And throughout the planet, seven Astral Veil Splits punch holes between dimensions.

Viridia is not simply a planet. It is a living myth, a cosmic crossroads, and the final proving ground of legends.

The Immortal Realms

Above & Below

Two thrones suspended beyond the Veil. Two eternal opposites. Both visible in Viridia's myths.

Virtue Immortal High Realm

Elyssara

Realm of Virtua

The celestial capital of the Virtue Immortals. Towers of light drift among astral clouds while immortal entities oversee the balance of creation. A kingdom of order, ascension, and spiritual harmony — the dwelling place of hope itself.

The Sacred

Living Sites of Viridia

Where philosophy, faith, and ancient power are kept.

World Tree Elyssianism

Tree of Elyssia

The oldest living structure on Viridia. A colossal divine tree connected directly to life energy, souls, and the spiritual consciousness of the planet. Some believe every humanoid soul briefly passes through its branches before rebirth.

Sky Bridge Naturism

Aurora Crown Bridge

A celestial bridge suspended above Viridia's upper atmosphere. Constructed naturally from astral stone, planetary roots, and living energy — Naturists revere it as the link between nature, civilization, and destiny. Sacred. Heavily protected.

Mountain Range Divine Protection

Dragonsong Peaks

Ancient dragon territory. Hidden temples, shard chambers, and celestial caves are buried in its slopes. The skies above are considered holy airspace. The Dragon Shard was once protected here — and may still be.

The Untamed

Where the Veil Thins

Lands of mystery, where myth and danger walk the same shore.

Bay & Ocean System Survival

Glasscurrent Bay & Oceania

Endless fog systems and deep astral waters hide veil fractures and submerged ruins. Massive creatures are rumored beneath the ocean floor. Few who explore here return — and fewer still return unchanged.

Volcanic Isles Ocularism

Cinderbay

A volcanic archipelago of black sand shores and astral fire rituals. Followers of Ocularism believe chaos and destruction are necessary for awakening. Despite its violence, communities here live in peace — through controlled chaos.

The Corruption Beneath

Khronexus

Beneath Viridia's western territories lies a hidden underground empire ruled by Khronis. Massive. Ancient. Labyrinthine. The seed of corruption planted at the world's foundation — a veil-manipulation center, an experimental astral prison, a civilization that should not exist.

The Present Age

Modern Viridia

A world rebuilding itself in the wake of three wars.

Capital Structure Order & Education

Heartplains Hall

The political and educational center of modern Viridia. Government capital. OVV headquarters. Diplomatic chamber and astral academy. Heartplains Hall represents unity, leadership, and the long reconstruction after the Great Wars. Most of Viridia's greatest leaders, scholars, and Astrals were trained within its walls.
