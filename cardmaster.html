Rules · Cardmaster Showdown

The gem bet. You and the AI each start with 27 gems. Gems are committed to your cards when their attacks land — never spent on use, never destroyed by misses.

  • A1 lands → 1 gem committed.
  • A2 lands → 2 gems committed.
  • A3 lands (dice 1d6 must beat opponent's) → 3 gems committed.
  • When a card falls, every gem committed to it is lost forever.

SPC. Free, one-time per match. Adds 25% of the card's SPC stat permanently to one chosen stat (HP / ATK / DEF / SPD).

DEF reduction. Five brackets (10 / 20 / 30 / 40 / 50%), capped at DEF 300.

Switch. Bring in any reserve any time. If your reserve's SPD beats the opponent's active, they also attack this turn (Pokemon momentum).

Revive. Cost = card tier (T1=1g … T5=5g). Returns at 50% HP, marked Risen ✦.

Win. Defeat all 9 of your opponent's cards, OR drain their total gems (pool + committed across living cards) to zero.
