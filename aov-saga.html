Chapter I — Realms

The Four Realms

Three starter worlds. One greater system. Each a doorway into the saga.

The First World

Origon

The origin point. Where reality was first shaped by the presence of Aenor — the Brightest One. Untouched. Unchanged. Closer to the source than any world that followed.

Bloodline World

Viridia

Cradle of the Veridae. Home of the Three Wars, the Olden powers, and the Immortals who walk between the divine and the mortal. Here, legend is law.

Gem-Forged Realm

Zyraxis

Where Gemlords reign and Zyrex burns at the core. A factionland of Rizing powers and beasts shaped by raw cosmic stone. The throne is never vacant — only contested.

Star System

Aethryx Expanse

The greater cosmos. A vault of orbits, rifts, and astral roads that bind every realm to a single timeline. To map the Expanse is to read the saga itself.

Chapter II — Eras

The Cosmic Timeline

Four ages. Three wars. One unbroken chronicle.

Era 01
The First War

When the Olden powers first turned upon one another and the heavens learned the meaning of fracture.

Era 02
The Second War

The bloodline wars. Crowns rose and fell beneath divine fire while mortal kingdoms became battlegrounds for immortal grudges.

Era 03
The Third War

A war of cosmic scale, fought across the Aethryx Expanse, where gemfire met astral steel and silence followed.

Era 04
The Mutagenesis Era

The age of becoming. New bloodlines, new beasts, new powers — born from the wreckage of all that came before.
